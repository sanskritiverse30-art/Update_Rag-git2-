Remote Work Policy

Eligibility:
Employees may request remote work after completing 90 days of employment, subject to manager approval and job suitability.

Approval Process:
Remote work requests must be submitted in writing to the employee's manager. The manager reviews the request and may consult HR before approving or denying it.

Work Expectations:
Remote employees must remain reachable during standard working hours and attend required meetings.

Equipment:
The company provides a laptop for approved remote employees. Employees are responsible for maintaining a secure internet connection.
