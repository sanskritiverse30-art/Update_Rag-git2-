Employee Handbook

Work Hours:
Standard office hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Employees must notify their supervisor if they expect to arrive late or be absent.

Paid Time Off:
Full-time employees receive 15 paid vacation days per year. Vacation requests should be submitted at least two weeks in advance and require manager approval.

Sick Leave:
Employees receive 8 sick days per year. Unused sick leave does not roll over unless required by local law.

Code of Conduct:
Employees are expected to maintain professionalism, respect coworkers, and comply with all workplace policies.
